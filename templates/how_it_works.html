The Process

1

Find a perfect match with just a few clicks.

We paired up with the best recruiters in the industry that offer reliable and satisfactory jobs. Our jobs cover housing, visas, flights, and many other benefits in all over Korea. explore different options and choose the best match.

2

Simplest application yet

Just fill out a single profile application once, which you can use it for every position you apply for. Say goodbye to nightmares of repeating your application every time.

3

Get connected with the recruiter

Once a recruiter accepts your application, you will be contacted by appointed recruiter. They will provide consulting for perfect match, interview rehearsal and get you a good interview.

4

Prepare your documents

At this stage, you already got an offer. For your smooth process, check out our document checklist

5

Time for adventure!

You are all set. Now pack your stuff and check in to your flight! Welcome to your new home and we hope the best for you.
