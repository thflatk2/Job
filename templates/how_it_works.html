Finding a job has never been this simple

The Process

1

Explore and find your perfect match

We paired up with the best recruiters in the industry that offer reliable and satisfactory jobs. Our jobs cover housing, visas, flights, and many other benefits in all over Korea. explore different options and choose the best match.

2

Simplest application yet

Loka uses common application. With Loka, you only need to fill out your basic profile once, which you can use it for every position you apply for. Say goodbye to nightmares of repeating your application every time.

3

Get connected with the recruiter

Once recruiters accept your application, you will be connected via the contact information you provided in the application. The recruiters will conduct further interviews if they see a potential match.

4

Prepare the documents

Congratulations on the offer! Loka is here to smooth out the process. Check out our documents checklist and details to sail your way to Korea

5

Accept the offer and move in!

You're all set. All you need to do is pack your stuffs and move in to your new home. I know moving to a whole new country can be very overwhelming, so Loka is here to help.
